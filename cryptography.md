## General cryptography

- [Cryptography and cryptanalysis course by Shafi Goldwasser at MIT](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL6ogFv-ieghe8MOIcpD6UDtdK-UMHG8oH). They follow this book: (Chapman & Hall_CRC Cryptography and Network Security Series) Jonathan Katz, Yehuda Lindell - Introduction to Modern Cryptography_ Principles and Protocols-Chapman and Hall_CRC (2007)
- [Stanford cryptography course at Stanford](https://crypto.stanford.edu/cs355/22sp/schedule/)
- [A Graduate Course in Applied Cryptography (Dan Boneh and Victor Shoup)](https://toc.cryptobook.us/book.pdf)
- [Handbook of applied cryptography (Alfred J. Menezes, Paul C. van Oorschot and Scott A. Vanstone)](https://cacr.uwaterloo.ca/hac/)
- [Joy of Cryptography - Mike Rosulek](https://joyofcryptography.com/)
